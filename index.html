About us

Who we are?

At EcoFlora Alliance, we dedicate ourselves to the conservation of rare and endangered plant species globally. Our goal is to foster awareness, offer support for ecological restoration, and actively engage in efforts to safeguard threatened vegetation from disappearing. With years of expertise, our team of horticulturists, environmental scientists, and passionate volunteers strives to preserve the beauty and diversity of our planet's botanical heritage.
